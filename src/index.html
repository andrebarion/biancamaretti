Biography

Bianca Maretti was born in the United States but began her conducting studies in Brazil, where she spents most of her life. She earned a bachelor's degree in orchestra and choir conducting from Unesp in 2017.

Later, She moved to Paris, obtaining bachelor's and master's degrees in musicology at Sorbonne Universités. Her research focused on women conductors, leading to a financed PhD thesis on gender equality measures in orchestral conducting.

Continuing her conducting studies, Bianca received a superior diploma in orchestra conducting from the École Normale de Musique de Paris in 2022. She also obtained a choral conducting diploma from the Conservatoire à Rayonnement Régional de Paris. Bianca have studied with various renowned teachers and participated in masterclasses worldwide.

Alongside her research, she teaches in the musicology department at Sorbonne.
